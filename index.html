Welcome to Cats & Soup: Fluffy Town, a heartwarming game where adorable feline friends gather to cook delicious soups and build a cozy village! Watch as cute kitties chop veggies, stir pots, and create a peaceful paradise in the middle of a magical forest.

Customize your fluffy town with charming decorations, expand your cat family, and unlock new recipes as you progress. Each cat has a unique personality, making every moment in the game feel special.

With soothing music, relaxing gameplay, and irresistibly cute animations, Cats & Soup: Fluffy Town is the perfect escape for cat lovers and casual gamers alike!
